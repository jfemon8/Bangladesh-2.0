Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Bangladesh has the potential for innovation, but its performance is lower than many other countries. However, there have been many innovations and initiatives in recent years.

Sustainability

Bangladesh is highly vulnerable to climate change and environmental degradation. Bangladesh is one of the most vulnerable countries to climate change.

Infrastructure

With Bangladesh on track to graduate from the UN's Least Developed Countries list in 2026, new infrastructure is helping open up the country's domestic and international trade.

Empowerment

Bangladesh has made significant progress in empowering women and girls in many areas, including education, employment, and political representation.
